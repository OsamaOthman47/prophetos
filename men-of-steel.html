“Men Of Steel”

Men of steel may melt and fold

They all get rusty as I'm shining gold

I'm the pen I'm the pencil and the ink

I'm the king and the knight I'm the sword

I'm the fire down the hell

I'm the ice up the nord

I'm the money in the bank

When you can't afford
