“Psyco-lovey”

As lots circles up the sphere

You are only what you fear

Arch of axes maybe sharp

But words in lips are sharpest gear

I whisper the quietest sound

The deaf of them, loud will hear

Hell is dim and dark and yet

Flames in dark do appear

Flames are melting when you’re far

And icing cold when you’re near

Stare of you can boil the sun

And leave the bravest one in fear

If you've liked a river once

Then you should've seen my tears

I aged of hearing silent void

Waiting long would ring my ear

Clocks they tick, in mine I found

Between two ticks it takes a year

Time could not keep to slow

When she drove fast to here

For four wheels I beg to stop

Hope She sights me back the rear

Her lips are dips of wine

And the sweat is the finest beer

That's my ink did say to you

And to you spilled that ink dear
