“Water”

It's raining in my eyes the water

To the beauty of Jesus' daughter

It's raising on earth an oceans

Making the sky way closer

Whales are big, seem too broad

My eyes seem to be broader

And if you seek the book of tears

To whom they read I'm an author

I'm ceasing two rivers on cheeks

The more I cease the more they offer

some are shamed with shame

I write my shame with honor

Floods, streams, and big seas

Shout and yell to make me smaller

She taught me how to cry

And that's what I taught her
